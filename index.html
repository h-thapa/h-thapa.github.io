What I Do

A multi-disciplinary skill set spanning quantitative finance, machine learning, and production-grade analytics engineering.

🤖

AI / Machine Learning

Designing and deploying ML pipelines — from supervised and unsupervised models to deep learning architectures — for real-world financial prediction, classification, and anomaly detection tasks.

📈

Quantitative Finance

Applying mathematical and statistical frameworks to pricing, risk measurement, factor modelling, and alpha generation. Comfortable with derivatives, fixed income, and equity instruments.

🏦

Asset & Wealth Management

Building analytical tools and decision-support systems for portfolio construction, performance attribution, ESG scoring, and client-facing reporting within buy-side environments.

🔮

Time Series Forecasting

Engineering accurate forecasting models (ARIMA, Prophet, LSTMs, Transformers) for price prediction, volatility estimation, macro variable projection, and demand sensing.

⚙️

Optimisation

Formulating and solving large-scale optimisation problems — mean-variance portfolio optimisation, risk parity, constrained allocation, and simulation-based methods (Monte Carlo, GA).

📊

Advanced Analytics

End-to-end data science workflows: data wrangling, exploratory analysis, feature engineering, model evaluation, and deployment of insight dashboards using Python and BI platforms.
